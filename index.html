GauSeva - Service to Cows, the highest virtue

🙏 Importance of Cows (Gau Mata) & Brahmin Service

Cows are the abode of all deities. Lord Krishna as Govardhanadhari and Gopala emphasizes GauSeva. Serving cows and Brahmins brings divine blessings, removes sins and ensures prosperity.

"गावो विश्वस्य मातरः" — ऋग्वेद

🐮 Cows are the mothers of the universe. They give selflessly and protect dharma.

"यस्य गावे च विप्राय च दद्यात्सर्वमेव तत्। स्वर्गलोकमवाप्नोति विष्णुलोकं स गच्छति॥"

🌸 One who donates cows and serves Brahmins attains heaven and finally reaches Vishnu's abode.
